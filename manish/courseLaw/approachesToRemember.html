Rules in Contracts, Torts, Criminal Law

  Contracts
  FORMATION QUESTIONS
  Raise issues of whether contract ever formed at all
1 Was there an OFFER?
2 ACCEPTANCE?
3 Manifestation of INTENT?
4 Was the contract for a LEGAL PURPOSE?
5 Supported by CONSIDERATION? (Seldom an issue on a UCC question)
   
  TERMS DISPUTES
  What were the terms of the contract?
1 Sufficiently CERTAIN that a court can enforce?
2 Was there a MODIFICATION supported by consideration?
3 Is a term or promissed performance a MATERIAL CONDITION?
4 Is a given term or promise INCLUDED or EXCLUDED from the agreement?
   
  THIRD PARTY QUESTIONS
  Who has the right to bring an action?
1 Were there THIRD PARTY BENEFICIARIES
2 Was there a VALID ASSIGNMENT?
3 Was there a VALID DELEGATION?
   
  DEFENSES QUESTIONS
  Focus on the defense issue
1 Statute of Frauds satisfied?
2 Lack of consideration?
3 Illegal purpose?
4 Rights of underage party?
5 Fraud? Duress?
   
  BREACH QUESTIONS
  Focus on the issue of who breached first?
1 Anticipatory breach
2 Major vs Minor breach
3 Waiver
   
  REMEDY QUESTIONS
  Raise issues of what each party can recover and how
1 SPECIFIC PERFORMANCE
2 RESTITUTION (quantam meruit concepts)?
3 RIGHT TO CURE?
4 COVER?
5 FAILURE TO MITIGATE?
6 ESTOPPEL?
7 SAVING DOCTRINES?
