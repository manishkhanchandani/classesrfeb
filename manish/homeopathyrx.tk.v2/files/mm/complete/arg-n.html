Walks and stands unsteadily, especially when he thinks himself unobserved.
Convulsions preceded by great restlessness.
Great weakness of lower extremities, with trembling; cannot walk with the eyes closed (Alum.).

MODALITIES

AGG.: Cold food; cold air; eating sugar; ice cream; UNUSUAL MENTAL EXERTION.
AMEL.: Open air; craves the wind blowing in his face; bathing with cold water.

RELATIONS

Nat-m. for the bad effects of cauterizing with nitrate of silver.
Coffea increases nervous headache.
Boys' complaints after using tobacco (Ars., Verat.).
Similar: to, Nat-m., Nit-ac, Lach., Aur., Cupr.
After Verat.; Lyc. follows well in flatulent dyspepsia.
