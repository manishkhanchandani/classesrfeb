BELLIS PERENNIS

(Bell-p.) 

(Daisy)

It acts upon the muscular fibers of the blood-vessels. Marked muscular soreness. Lameness, as if sprained. Venous congestion, due to mechanical causes. First remedy for injuries to the deeper tissues, after major surgical work. Results of injuries to nerves with intense soreness and intolerance to cold bathing. After gout, debility of limbs. Traumatism of the pelvic organs, auto-traumatism, express the condition calling for this remedy; ill effects from masturbation. Excellent remedy for sprains and bruises. Complaints due to cold food or drink when the body is heated, and in affections due to cold wind. Externally, in nevi. Acne. BOILS ALL OVER. SORE, BRUISED FEELING IN THE PELVIC REGION. Exudations, stasis, swelling, come within the range of this remedy. Rheumatic symptoms. Does not vitiate the secretions. "It is a princely remedy for old laborers, especially gardeners." (Burnett.)

Head
Vertigo in elderly people. Headache from occiput to top of the head. Forehead feels contracted. BRUISED SORENESS. Itching around scalp and over the back, worse from hot bath and bed.

Abdomen
SORENESS OF ABDOMINAL WALLS AND OF UTERUS. Stitches in spleen, sore, enlarged (splenomegaly). Yellow, painless diarrhoea, foul odor, worse at night. Bloated; rumbling in bowels.

Genitalia-Female
Breasts and uterus engorged. Varicose veins during pregnancy. DURING PREGNANCY INABILITY TO WALK. Abdominal muscles lame. UTERUS FEELS SORE, AS IF SQUEEZED.

Extremities
Joints sore, muscular soreness (rheumatism). Itching on back and flexor surfaces of thighs. Pain down anterior of thighs. Wrist feels contracted as if from elastic band around joint. Sprains with great soreness. RAILWAY SPINE.

Sleep
Wakes up early in morning and cannot get to sleep again.

Skin
BOILS. Ecchymosis, swelling, very sensitive to touch. Venous congestion due to mechanical causes. Varicose veins with bruised sore feeling. Exudations and swellings. Acne.

Modalities
AGG.: LEFT SIDE; hot bath and warmth of bed; before storms; cold bathing; cold wind.

Relationship
Compare: ARNICA,  ARS.,  STAPH., HAM., BRYONIA, VANADIUM (degenerative states).

Dose
Tincture to third potency.

BELLIS PERENNIS (Bell-p.)

Injuries on deeper tissues.

 

Soreness of muscles due to injuries or surgery. Extravasation of blood, ecchymosis. Affections of mammae from contrusion. Venous congestion.Varicosities.

Soreness of pelvic region. Soreness of abdominal walls. Soreness of uterus during pregnancy. Uterus feels as if squeezed.

MODALITIES:

Aggravation: cold applications, hot bathing, before storm.
