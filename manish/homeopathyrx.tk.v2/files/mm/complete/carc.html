CARCINOSINUM (Carc.)

 

 

The vulnerability and emotional oversensitivity are the characteristics of this remedy. The patient since early age is very proper, disciplined, yielding, wants to please everybody in order to gain appreciation and love from others. They’re so sensitive that the impact of an even slight remark is disproportionate on them.

We often find a family history of cancer or diabetes.

 

 

VERY SENSITIVE TO REPRIMANDS, PLEASING OTHERS, FAMILY HISTORY OF CANCER

 

 

MENTAL

 

EASILY OFFENDED, OVERSENSITIVE TO CRITICISM AND REPRIMANDS. Fear of humiliation. SYMPATHETIC, YIELDING DISPOSITION.

Lack of self-confidence, WANTS TO PLEASE EVERYBODY.

Craving for affection.

Timid. Fastidious. Sentimental. Passionate.

 

History of suppression and guilty feelings. Sad but cannot weep. Silent grief.

 

Desires and ameliorated by dancing and music.

Loves the excitement of thunder storms and lightnings.

 

Anxiety about health. Fears cancer.

Horrible things, sad stories affect them profoundly.

 

Children

1st type (usual): obedient, responsible, disciplined in order to please parents.

2nd type (rare): insensitive, dissatisfied, self-centered, misbehaving.

 

 

PHYSICAL

 

LACK OF REACTION, e.g. ABSENCE OF FEVER.

 

Past history of cancer or family history of cancer or diabetes. Complaints develops at the time of puberty.

 

Complexion is dark, earthy, “café au lait”. Often the patient has dark moles.

Blueness of conjunctiva, esp. in children.

 

Insomnia. Child must be rocked. Sleepless from thoughts. Early waking at 4am.

Warm blooded and generally worse from heat.

Sexual desire increased, too frequent or early masturbation.

Sleeps in knee-chest position (Med.).

Frequent winking of the eyes.

Cough from a tickle in the throat. Cough < yawning, talking, laughing.

 

 

MODALITIES

 

AGG.: afternoon from 13:00-18:00 or esp. 17:00-18:00.

AMEL.: sea, seashore, evening, short sleep.

DES.: chocolate, salt, fat, fat ham, spicy foods, alcoholic drinks, cold drinks.

AVERS.: fat.

 

CARCINOSINUM

(Carc.)

(A Nosode from Carcinoma)

It is claimed that CARCINOSINUM acts favorably and modifies all cases in which either a history of carcinoma can be elicited, or symptoms of the disease itself exist (J. H. Clarke, M. D.).
Carcinoma of the mammary glands with great pain and induration of glands; of uterus, with offensive discharges, hemorrhage and pain are greatly relieved.
Indigestion, accumulation of gas in stomach and bowels; rheumatism cancerous cachexia.

Mind
Intellectual torpor. Thinks with difficulty; indifference; apathy. Worse by conversation. Tendency to suicide. Dislikes consolation. Children have great fear, sensitive to reprimands (MED.), but is sympathetic to others (PHOS.).

Relationship
Compare: BUFO; CON., PHYT., ASTER.

Dose
Thirtieth and 200th potency, a dose at night or less frequently.
