MENYANTHES (Meny.)

 

 

A REMEDY FOR CERTAIN HEADACHES, INTERMITTENT FEVER

 

 

MENTAL

 

Confusion > open air.

Discontent. Restlessness.

Forsaken feeling.

Fear of being alone with headaches.

Dwells on disagreeable occurrences (Nat-m.).

Anxiety about heart.

 

 

PHYSICAL

 

Headaches: as if weight on vertex pressing down, better pressing hard with hand in the same direction, better stooping (lgn., Cina), worse ascending (Calc.).

Cold hands and feet (Calc., Sep.), cold tip of nose, tension in root of nose.

Coldness of abdomen.

Icy cold toes and fingers extending to knees and elbows.

Fever: Cold stage predominates. Coldness of the tip of the nose.

Thirstless.

Twitchings. Diabetes.

 

 

MODALITIES

 

AGG.: rest and lying down, evening.

AMEL.: hard pressure with hands (China), motion.

 

MENYANTHES

(Meny.)

(Buck-bean)

A remedy for certain headaches, intermittent fever. Coldness of the abdomen. Twitchings. Sensation of tension and compression. Fidgets and urinary difficulties in women. Diabetes.

Head
Pressing in the vertex; BETTER, HARD PRESSURE WITH THE HAND. Pressing-together pain. Weight pressing on the brain with every step on ascending. Pain from the nape over the whole brain; better, stooping, sitting; worse, going upstairs. Cracking in the jaw and twitching of facial muscles.

Stomach
No thirst at any time. Ravenous hunger; passing away after eating a little. Desire for meat. Sensation of coldness extending up to the oesophagus.

Abdomen
Distended and full; increased by smoking tobacco. Coldness of the abdomen.

Extremities
ICY COLDNESS OF HANDS AND FEET. Cramp-like pain. As soon as patient lies down, LEGS JERK AND TWITCH.

Fever
Coldness predominates; felt most acutely in the abdomen and legs and tip of the nose.

Modalities
AGG.: during rest, ascending.

AMEL.: pressure on the affected part, stooping, motion.

Relationship
Compare: Caps., Puls., Calc., Ph-ac., Sang.

Antidote: Camph..

Dose
Third to thirtieth potency.

MENYANTHES

(Meny.)

BUCK BEAN (GENTIANACEAE)

MENTAL

Anxiety about the heart as if some evil was impending.

PHYSICAL

Complaints from abuse of cinchona and quinine.
TENSION: in root of nose; in arms, hands, fingers; in skin, as if several sizes too small and was crowded into it by force.

Head
Headache: PRESSING IN VERTEX from above downwards, > DURING HARD PRESSURE WITH HAND (Verat.); as of a HEAVY WEIGHT pressing upon the head at every step (Cac, Glon., Lach.) ; < ascending (Calc.) ; often with icy coldness of hands and feet (Calc., Sep.).

Fever
Fevers, in which the cold stage predominates; coldness felt most acutely in abdomen and legs.

MODALITIES

AGG: During rest; lying down.
AMEL: Pressure on affected part.

RELATIONS

Compare: Cact., Calc., Gels., Sep., Mag-m., Par.
Follow well: Caps., Lach., Lyc., Puls., Rhus-t., Verat.
