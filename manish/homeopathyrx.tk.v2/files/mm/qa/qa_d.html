Remedies

Digitalis

1. This is a major heart remedy. What are the common heart indications?

2. What are the main worse for modalities of this remedy?

3. Describe how the urinary symptoms might present.

Answers

1. Pulse is feeble, SLOW, intermittent and irregular

Any motion can cause the pulse to become rapid and irregular

Movement leads to syncope (fainting) in heart disease.

Sudden sensation as if the heart stood still

Least movement = palpitations

Cyanosis (bluishness) of the face

Any dropsy caused by heart problems

2. Exertion and movement

Night and first thing in the morning

Raising up

Lying on the left side

3. Urination is difficult with sensations of burning and constriction as if the urethra was too narrow.

Constant urge to urinate and dribbling of urine.

Throbbing pain at the neck of the bladder as if a straw were pushed up and down the urethra < night.

Retains a sensation of a full bladder even after urination.
