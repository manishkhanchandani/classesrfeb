Remedies

Gelsemium

1. What are the characteristic mental/emotional symptoms of this remedy?

2. How do the arms and legs, or body in general feel?

3. What is the main better for modality?

4. What are the main keynotes for this remedy?

5. What are the main headache symptoms for this remedy?

6. What are the main worse for modalities?

Answers

1. Confusion of mind.

Fear of falling - child being carried will grab the person, grabbing the sides of the cot or bed

Fear of appearing in public - stage fright (Arg nit also)

Anticipatory anxiety - especially of any performance or the spotlight being on them, brings on diarrhoea

Fears that if they stop moving the heart will stop beating (compare with Digitalis - if they move the heart will stop beating)

Dullness of the mind - feels drowsy a lot of the time

2. They feel very heavy, all movement is an effort. There may be lack of coordination of movement or a degree of paralysis of the limbs. The head can feel heavy too - holding it up is an effort. They might seem intoxicated as they move.

3. Better for profuse urination, especailly the headache.

4. Paralysis, dullness, numbness.

5. Headache starts in the occiput or nape of the neck.

Headache progresses of the top of the head to settle over the eye (often the right)

Headache with blurred vision and dizziness.

< 10 am

Headache > profuse urination

6. Heat of sun or summer - e.g summar 'flu'

Talking about ailments or focus drawn to their problems

Mental activity and emotional upsets

Tobacco smoke

Before a thunderstorm.
