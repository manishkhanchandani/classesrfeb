NUX VOMICA (Nux-v.)

The central idea in this remedy is the irritation of the nervous system.

2 main symptoms of this remedy are:

1. OVEREXCITABILITY OF NERVOUS SYSTEM, 2. IRRITABILITY

1. Overexcitability of nervous system:
The individual is excited, impatient, quick to act and very irritable. It is often used in states of exhaustion from over-working, where the nervous system cannot tolerate the slightest stimulus. The noise, the light, the traffic, every little stress is intolerable.

2. Irritability:
The irritability is found and is a leading characteristic during acute diseases.

On the physical level, this over excitability manifests in twitching, jerking, headaches but primarily in the sensitivity of the stomach.
